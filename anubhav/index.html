ANUBHAV 3.0


30th Sep'18

What is ANUBHAV?

Anubhav 3.0 was the third edition of the annual art workshop for the lesser privileged children conducted by SPIC MACAY NIT Durgapur Chapter in collaboration with BMEP. The kids were taught various origami skills by our volunteers and they managed to produce some amazing creations, ranging from paper boats to beautiful ducks and bags. The smile on their faces and the glistening hope in their eyes at the end of the day was simply heartwarming and worth all the efforts that we took. Anubhav 3.0 was a grand success and we look forward to making Anubhav even bigger and grander in the years to come.
